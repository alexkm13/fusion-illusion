Our Food | Our Story

Our Story

Fusion Illusion started out as an idea proposed by three college students around a dining hall table. We never expected to grow into the nationwide franchise we are today. Our original restuarant based in Boston served quality east asian fusion food aiming to revolutionize the culinary experience through a collaboration of cultures. It was during this time we introduced our world famous Melting Snow Mochi Tart and other beloved staples in our menu. Fusion Illusion has come a long way since our humble beginnings and we are excited to show you our future.

Our Commitment to Quality

Our expansion into a nationwide franchise has not diminished our commitment to seasonal and quality produce in our dishes. That is why you will find differing menus in our select locations around the nation. We challenge the great minds and chefs at Fusion Illusion to make the best use of local ingredients in showcasing what east asian fusion cuisine could really be.

Our Mission

Our mission to our customers remain the same as when we started. To serve revolutionary high quality east asian fusion food. We have adhered to this mission by sourcing our produce and meats from local farms and preparing it every morning before opening. In doing this, we not only are able to provide the customers with high quality foods, but also give back to the community who has supported us all the way.
